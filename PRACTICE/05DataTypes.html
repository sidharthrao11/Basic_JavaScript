    PREMITIVE DATATYPES

    NON-PREMITIVE DATAtYPES

Q. What is the diffrence between Undefined and Not Defined?
NOT DEFINED - A variable that has not been declared at all is not defined.
(variable declair hi nahi kiya gaya.)
UNDEFINED - It indicates that a variable has been declared but not given a value,
(variable declaired hai par value assign nahi karta.)
